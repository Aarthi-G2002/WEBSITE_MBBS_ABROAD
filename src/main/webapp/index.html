Why Study MBBS Abroad?

Studying MBBS abroad offers students globally recognized degrees with high academic standards. Many countries provide affordable tuition fees, making medical education more accessible. Universities abroad have advanced infrastructure, modern research facilities, and well-equipped hospitals. Students gain international exposure, experiencing diverse medical cases and cultures. Most universities offer English-medium courses, eliminating language barriers for foreign students. The admission process is simple, with no entrance exams required in many top destinations. Global networking opportunities allow students to build strong career connections worldwide. Graduates have options to work, settle, or specialize abroad with greater ease.

Admission Process & Eligibility

Follow these simple steps to apply for an MBBS program abroad. Ensure you meet the eligibility criteria before proceeding.

Step 1: Check Eligibility

Candidates must have completed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology, scoring a minimum of 50%.

Step 2: Choose University & Country

Select a university based on budget, ranking, and language preferences. Popular choices include Russia, Philippines, and Georgia.

Step 3: Submit Application

Fill out the application form online and submit required documents like academic transcripts, passport, and photos.

Step 4: Receive Admission Letter

Once selected, you will receive an admission letter from the university, confirming your seat.

Step 5: Apply for Visa

Submit the admission letter, passport, and financial proof to apply for a student visa.

Step 6: Pay Tuition Fees

Once the visa is approved, pay the initial tuition fees directly to the university.

Step 7: Fly to Your Destination

Book your flight, arrange accommodation, and attend the university orientation program.
